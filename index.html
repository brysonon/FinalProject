A Brief Introduction

Welcome to my website. My name is Bryson Phillip On, and I am a full-time student at Brigham Young University and a part-time crime-fighting, riddle-solving, spandex-wearing Gotham City superhero. Enjoy learning a few fun facts about me below. Clicking on each of the sections will take you to a different site.

I lov-I mean I am Batman.

I'm a single pringle. Except for my butler, Alfred, I ride solo. In my freetime (when I'm not kicking butt) I like playing Fortnite and solving riddles from The Riddler.

I really like soccer.

Believe it or not, superheroes can play sports too. I've played soccer since I was an itty-bitty baby, so the sport has a special place in my heart. I once got red-carded for slicing someone's pants with my Batarang. I also beat Superman in a penalty shoot-out.

Mexico is my favorite place I've lived.

Back when I still couldn't fit into my spandex, I lived in Mexico for three years. Although I missed the bustling streets of Gotham, I developed a love for Mexican culture. As a bonus, there was tons of corruption and crime to tackle. The Mexicans always seemed to confuse me with the Bat Man, even though I told them my alias was one word.
